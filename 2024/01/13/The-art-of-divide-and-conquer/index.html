It’s a way to solve big problems by breaking them into smaller problems and tackle each of them. I had little feelings and I didn’t dive deep or think big about it, after solving asignment questions with the technique.

Over the years, I slowly realized that this is not only about computer science. Instead, I see its application in different domains.
